# Imprisoned

**A dark 2D adventure about escaping captivity and surviving hostile environments.**
![Gameplay](gameplay.gif)
---

## The Story

![Gameplay GIF](gameplay.gif)

You wake up in a place you don’t remember entering.  
The air is heavy, the halls are silent, and something feels wrong.

There’s only one clear objective:

**Get out.**

Explore the environment, avoid dangers, and uncover a path to freedom —  
if one exists.

---

## About the Game

**Imprisoned** is a 2D atmospheric adventure built in **Unity**, focused on exploration, tension, and environmental storytelling.

Players must carefully navigate hostile spaces, avoid enemies, and interact with the environment to progress.

The game emphasizes atmosphere and pacing, encouraging players to observe their surroundings and plan their moves.

---

## Gameplay

- Explore mysterious environments  
- Avoid enemies and dangerous obstacles  
- Solve environmental challenges to progress  
- Discover hidden paths and safe routes

**Stay quiet. Stay alert. Escape.**
